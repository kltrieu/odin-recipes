Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cooking oil
  • Chicken Stock
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Pork Shoulder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fish Sauce
  • Coconut Milk
  • White Rice

Directions

  1. Cut pork into 1 inch cubes and season with salt and pepper. Brown in saute pan and remove
  2. Combine equal parts flour and cooking oil to pan and stir to create a roux
  3. Add sliced onions, carrots,potatoes, and garlic to roux. Saute until aromatic
  4. Add chicken stock to pan. Make sure to add in slowly to prevent lumps
  5. Add pork back into pan then allow to simmer, anywhere from 2-4 hours
  6. Add additional ingredients as wanted, such as fish sauce and coconut milk
  7. Serve with rice. Enjoy :)
